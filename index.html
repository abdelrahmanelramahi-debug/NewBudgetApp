Welcome to Finance Command

Track spending, weekly allowance, and bank balance in one place.

Set up your budget in a few steps. You can change anything later in Settings.

Step 1 of 6

Currency

What currency do you use? You can change this in Settings.

Step 2 of 6

Monthly income

Your total monthly income (after tax). We use this to suggest savings, weekly allowance, and categories. You can set this to 0 if you only track expenses.

Please enter a valid number (0 or more).

Step 3 of 6

What’s in your bank right now?

Roughly how much do you have (e.g. in your main account or wallet)? We’ll make your Bank Balance match this. You can fine-tune in Profile later. Skip for 0.

Step 4 of 6

Categories

Template adds more categories; minimal keeps only Savings, Weekly Allowance, Food, and Car. You can add or edit in Budget Plan later.

Step 5 of 6

Weekly allowance

Your income is split into 4 weeks. Pre-fill all 4 weeks with your weekly amount so you can switch and transfer between them?

Step 6 of 6

You’re all set

Check your choices below. You can add or edit categories in Budget Plan and change income or currency in Settings.
